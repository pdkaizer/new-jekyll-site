Some examples of my work

Catholic Relief Services

Until recently, I was the digital director for Catholic Relief Services. My team managed front and back end development, online branding, user experience, information architecture and email marketing for all of the CRS websites.

Role: Digital strategy, Online fund-raising, Front-end development, email marketing, SEO, PPC

So Charmed

So Charmed is the jewlery website of Jodi Bloom, a terrific graphic designer, story teller and talented designer of off beat jewelry. Each piece has a story behind it.

Jodi designed the interface and I developed the Wordpress Theme and publishing routine. Using the Advanced Custom Fields plugin and the Simple Paypal Shopping Cart plugin made for a simple and easy administrative user experience to publish new pieces, tell the story behind them and make them available for purchase via Paypal.

Role: Front-end development, Wordpress themeing

University of Maryland School of Theatre, Dance and Performance Studies

Drupal 6 website for the University of Maryland School of Theatre, Dance and Performance Arts.

Role: Front-end development, Drupal themeing, Information architecture

David Snyder Photography

David Snyder is a photographer & photojournalist for non-profit organizations. He has lived and traveled al over the world. I designed and built his website as well as set him up in Campaign Monitor so he can send out html email newsletters.
